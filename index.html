Hello, I'm Giovana

A passionate enthusiast yoga instructor helping people to find balance and metal clarity while they experience stress reduction, pain relief, better posture, improved sleep, strength and flexibility.

Embracing yoga was a natural transition for me, especially after becoming a mother of two. My scientific background in Neuroscience gave me a unique understanding about meditation and pranayamas which I passionately share through my sessions.

I'm known for my communicative and friendly nature are shown throughout my sessions infused with energy and joy. In Sydney, I offer yoga classes for both adults and kids and I organise enriching workshops and retreats.

Join me on this journey to balance and happiness through yoga!

For Kids

Yoga for Kids in Childcare & Preschools

Our fun and imaginative kids yoga sessions bring movement, mindfulness, and storytelling into your centre. Through age-appropriate poses, games, and breathwork, children develop balance, coordination, and emotional awareness—all while having a great time! Each class is tailored to support early learning and self-regulation in a playful and nurturing way. Encouraging to be creative and to relax with positive affirmations.

Yoga and Activities

For schools, preschools and childcares; baby yoga (1-2 years old) and kids yoga (2-5 years old).

Why YogiClub?

We believe that the early years are the perfect time to nurture healthy bodies, calm minds, and joyful hearts. Gio, the founder and teacher behind YogiClub, brings a unique blend of expertise in movement, mindfulness, and storytelling—along with years of experience as a yoga instructor and a mum of two. Her passion for children's well-being shines through in every class, where yoga becomes an adventure filled with fun poses, themed stories, breathing exercises, and creative play.

About Our Program

YogiClub's program blends beautifully into early childhood education, supporting emotional development, confidence, creativity, and self-regulation. Our 10-week - Term program aligns with NSW school terms and requires no extra planning from educators—we bring everything needed for a joyful and enriching experience that supports the whole child.
