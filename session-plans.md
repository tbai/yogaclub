# Yogi Club — Session Plans Created

## 1. Dino Dinner — Healthy Eating Theme
- **Created:** 09/03/2026
- **Theme:** How important it is to eat clean and healthy
- **Structure:** 30 min session
  1. Warm Up (5 min) — Sun Salutation Song by MiniPadmini
  2. Story Time (10 min) — "Dino Dino" interactive book (bike legs, scooter legs, W2 skateboard)
  3. Action Song (8 min) — Yoga Freeze Dance by Yoga Guppy (Rashmi Ramesh)
     - Freeze poses: Tree, Triangle, Warrior 2 (Surfer), Airplane, Dancer, Waterfall, Chair
     - Final round: Statue Challenge — who can freeze completely still!
  4. Relaxation (7 min) — Rainbow scarves + star props + Rainbow song
- **PDF:** dino-dinner-session-plan.pdf
- **Age adaptations:** Nest (0-1), Fledglings (1-2), Stage 2 (2-3), Stage 3 (3-4), Stage 4 (4-5)
